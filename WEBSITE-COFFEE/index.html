About Us

Welcome to Cozy Cup Café, where every sip is a hug in a cup! Located in the heart of Chennai, Cozy Cup Café is your go-to destination for premium coffee, delicious bites, and unforgettable experiences. Since our inception, we've been dedicated to crafting the perfect cup of coffee using carefully sourced beans, blended with love and brewed to perfection. Whether you're looking for a cozy corner to unwind, a lively spot to catch up with friends, or a productive workspace fueled by caffeine, Cozy Cup Café offers the perfect ambiance to suit your mood.
